# Privacy Policy for WorkFlow

**Last Updated:** [Date]

**Effective Date:** [Date]

## Introduction

WorkFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application WorkFlow (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Work Entry Data

We collect the following information that you voluntarily enter when you use the App:

- **Work Entries:** Dates and times of work entries, hours worked, hourly rates, client names, and calculated earnings.
- **Reminders:** If you set reminders for entries, reminder dates and times are stored to deliver notifications (with your permission).

### 2. App Preferences and Settings

We store minimal data to provide and improve the App:

- Theme and display preferences
- Notification settings
- Language preference
- Export/import history is handled locally on your device

### 3. Device and Permissions

When you use certain features, the App may access:

- **Notifications:** To send you reminders about your work entries (with your permission)

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or analytics data.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality:**
   - Create, store, and manage your work entries and earnings
   - Sync your data across your devices (when iCloud is enabled)
   - Send you notifications and reminders (when permitted)
   - Calculate statistics, summaries, and export data (PDF, CSV)

2. **Improve the App:**
   - Your data is processed locally; we do not send your work entry data to our servers for analytics

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- Work entry data, client names, rates, and earnings are stored on your device
- Deleted entries (trash) are stored locally until you permanently delete them or empty the trash

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

- If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit service
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- CloudKit data is stored in your private iCloud account and is not accessible to us
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the App settings

### Data Security

- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Export and import files are generated and read only on your device unless you choose to share them

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **Service Providers:**
   - **Apple Services:** We use Apple's services (iCloud, CloudKit) for optional sync. Their use of data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

2. **Legal Requirements:**
   - We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities

### Export and Import

- Export (PDF, CSV) creates files on your device; you control whether and with whom you share those files
- Import reads files you select; we do not upload or transmit imported data to our servers

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### Apple Services

- **iCloud/CloudKit:** For optional data synchronization across devices
- **Local Notifications:** For reminders (processed on your device)

We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party AI services.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

1. **Access:** You can access all your data through the App
2. **Export:** You can export your data (PDF or CSV) from the App settings
3. **Delete:** You can delete individual entries, restore or permanently delete from the Deleted Entries (trash), or delete all data from the App settings
4. **Disable Features:** You can disable iCloud sync or notifications at any time
5. **Data Portability:** You can export your data at any time and use it outside the App

### Managing Permissions

You can manage App permissions through your device's Settings:
- Notification permissions (for reminders)

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as you use the App
- If you delete the App, your local data is removed from your device
- If you disable iCloud sync, your CloudKit data may be retained according to Apple's data retention policies
- You can delete all your data at any time through the App settings
- Deleted entries (trash) can be restored or permanently deleted by you

## International Data Transfers

- If you use iCloud sync, your data may be stored and processed by Apple in various locations worldwide, subject to Apple's data processing practices
